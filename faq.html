General Questions

What is North Point Capital Investments?

North Point Capital Investments provides investment management, financial planning, and wealth management solutions for individuals and businesses.

How do I open an account?

Click the Open Account button, complete the registration form, verify your email, and sign in to access your investor dashboard.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes. We use secure authentication and encryption practices to help protect your personal information and account.

Who can invest with North Point Capital Investments?

Individuals, businesses, and eligible organizations can open an investment account, subject to our account verification requirements and applicable regulations.

Do I need investing experience?

No. Whether you're a first-time investor or an experienced investor, our team can help you choose an investment strategy that aligns with your financial goals.

Investment Questions

What investment plans are available?

We offer Starter, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and VIP investment options designed for different financial objectives and investment amounts.

Are investment returns guaranteed?

No. Investment returns are not guaranteed. The value of investments can rise or fall depending on market conditions. Any examples shown on this website are for illustration only.

Can I monitor my investments?

Yes. After signing in, you'll be able to view your investment portfolio, account information, and transaction history from your dashboard.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click the Forgot Password? link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

How is my account protected?

We use secure authentication, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and account verification measures to help protect your account and personal information.

Can I update my personal information?

Yes. After logging into your dashboard, you can update eligible profile information. Some changes may require identity verification.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How do I fund my investment account?

After your account is approved, you can follow the funding instructions available in your investor dashboard. Available payment methods depend on your region.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary depending on the payment method, verification status, and internal review requirements.

Is identity verification required?

Yes. We may require identity verification to help protect your account and comply with applicable financial regulations before certain transactions are processed.

Investor Dashboard

What can I do from my dashboard?

Your dashboard allows you to view your portfolio, monitor investments, review transaction history, update your profile, and manage your account.

Can I access my dashboard on mobile?

Yes. The dashboard is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.
